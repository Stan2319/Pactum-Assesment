# Pricing — Pactum

Pactum is an AI-powered hiring assessment platform. Candidates complete real work tasks using AI tools; Pactum records every prompt and decision and scores the session automatically.

## Starter

- Price: $99/month
- Best for: Teams hiring 1-5 roles per month
- Active assessments: Up to 5
- Candidates: Unlimited
- Task types: Email, code review, written analysis, slide deck
- AI scoring and rubrics: Included
- Custom rubric builder: Not included
- Support: Email support

## Professional

- Price: $549/month
- Best for: Teams scaling hiring across departments
- Active assessments: Unlimited
- Candidates: Unlimited
- Task types: Email, code review, written analysis, slide deck
- AI scoring and rubrics: Included
- Custom rubric builder: Included
- Support: Priority support

## Enterprise

- Price: Custom (contact hello@pactum.so)
- Best for: Large organizations with compliance or SSO requirements
- Active assessments: Unlimited
- Candidates: Unlimited
- Task types: All types + custom
- AI scoring and rubrics: Included
- Custom rubric builder: Included
- SSO: Included
- SLA: Included
- Dedicated account manager: Included
- Support: Dedicated account manager

## Notes

- All plans include unlimited candidates per assessment
- Private beta pricing — may change at general availability
- Annual billing options available on request
- Free trial available during private beta
